# UNOUN — Target Audience & Marketing

## Primary Audience

**Who:** Women, 25–45 years old, Russia
**Income:** Above average (80,000–150,000 ₽/month)
**Lifestyle:** Active homemakers, young mothers, career women who value a clean home
**Geography:** Major Russian cities (Moscow, SPB, cities 500k+)
**Mindset:** Values quality over price, researches before buying, influenced by aesthetics

## Core Problem

- Home cleaning takes too much time and effort
- Chemical cleaners are harmful for children and pets
- Regular mops don't clean grout, glass, upholstery, and clothing all at once
- Multiple single-purpose cleaning devices clutter storage
- Cheap devices break quickly — money wasted twice

## How UNOUN Solves It

- 12-in-1 system replaces mop, steamer, garment steamer, window cleaner, scrubber
- 120°C steam kills 99.9% of bacteria — no chemicals needed (safe for children & pets)
- 1500W heats up in 25 seconds — fast, no waiting
- 2-year warranty — confidence in long-term quality
- Premium German-technology aesthetic — not ashamed to store it visibly

## Value Proposition

**"One device. 12 tasks. Perfectly clean home without effort."**
